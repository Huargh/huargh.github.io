About

My name is Matthias Aigner, 32 years old. I live in Vienna, Austria. The primary purpose of this pen is to present my completed challenges from Free Code Camp.
My day job as an SAP Consultant does involve (ABAP-)programming to some extent, but programming in ABAP couldn't be more different from the web environment.
In the last years on my job, I spent less and less time in a development environment. However, I still fancy working with code & related technologies, so I decided to try to pick up some web development skills in my spare time.
As you might have experienced, learning a new language/skillset takes practice, and especially regular practice. Finding time for that after a regular workday isn't always easy. So if you'd like to send me some motivation, please feel free to contact me. Also, if you'd like to hear more about my professional experience, what I do besides sitting in front of a screen, my famous Pichlstoana-recipe or what the hell Pichlstoana is, let me know!
